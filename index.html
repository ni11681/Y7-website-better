Why should we learn about space?



Throughout history humanity has been enamoured with the cosmos,
to us it represent's something mysterious, something more...
And even now in the the 21st century our universe still holds its secrets and our technology is not yet great enough to uncover them.
Inevitably some will say it's useless, that we ignore the problems of our own planet for those of the cosmos,
but nearly every innovation in technology has been because of our reach into the universe.
Think on the space race two countries fighting not with weapons but with minds perhaps even the answer to the heating climate lies in humans living abroad...
In the vast expanse of the cosmos.




BLACK HOLES


Black holes are formed when stars collapse after a supernova these only occur when the star is big enough and has enough mass.
They have a huge amount of matter and are so heavy that they can slow time and stop light. Black holes have three main parts,
the acretion disk, wich is made up of matter circling the black hole from stars or planets it has eaten,
the event horizon, the point of no return where nothing can escape not even light & the singularity
a point so dense and small that the laws of physics as we know them break down.
