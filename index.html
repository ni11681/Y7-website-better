CRAZY SPACE

This website is about the wonders of space
and why we should work to understand and appreciate them.



Why should we learn about space?



Throughout history humanity has been enamoured with the cosmos to us it represent's something mysterious, something more... And even now in the the 21st century our universe still holds its secrets and our technology is not yet great enough to uncover them. Inevitably some will say it's useless, that we ignore the problems of our own planet for those of the cosmos, but nearly every innovation in technology has been because of our reach into the universe. Think on the space race two countries fighting not with weapons but with minds perhaps even the answer to the heating climate lies in humans living abroad... In the vast expanse of the cosmos.

CRAB NEBULA

The Crab Nebula is the shattered remnant of a massive star that ended its life in a supernova explosion.
Nearly a thousand years old,
the supernova was noted in the constellation of Taurus by Chinese astronomers in the year 1054 AD.
